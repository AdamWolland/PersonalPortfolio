Project 1: Photography

I love to take photos of extreme nature. On a trip to Edinburgh I captured this bird flying through a wave hitting the cliffs. I edited the photograph in Adobe Photoshop and chose a high contrast black and white edit to emphasize the extreme conditions.

Project 2: Fontfamily

Made Canvas is a serif font with contrasts. The font family contains three versions; light, medium and black. In all the different versions the hairline is fairly thin. Can be used for magazines or branding for instance.
